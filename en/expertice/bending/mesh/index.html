WHAT IS MESH?
Mesh applications, which plays a major role in improving aesthetics, have taken their place among the important aspects of the construction sector. Mesh applications are used in many areas such as landscaping, furniture, exterior façade, and decoration.

As SCZ Metal, our experience and our large selection of high-tech machinery are what sets us apart from the competition and provides us with the opportunity to create beautiful Mesh designs.
