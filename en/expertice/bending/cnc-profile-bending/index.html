CNC Profile Bending
Our CNC Profile Bending machines add strength to our expertise in Bending.
WHAT IS CNC Profile Bending?
The CNC Profile Bending process allows the shaping of the designs of your project through methods such as pipe and aluminum bends, circle gears, spiral ladders, conical bends, sheet bends, and corner angle bends.

Solmaz Aluminum, with its 35 years of experience in belt bending and CNC-controlled machines, can bend aluminum facade profiles, stainless steel, brass box profiles, pipes, and corners to desired sizes.
