OUR EXPERTISE
Welding
One of our expertise in production is Welding.
Stainless Steel. Aluminum. Iron. Brass. Copper.
With various welding machines, we are more than capable of welding stainless steel, aluminum, iron, brass, and copper with ease. One of our specialties include impeccable workmanship in both aluminum and stainless steel “letter” production.
WHAT IS THE POLISHING AND SATINATION?
Polishing: is the process of giving a shiny and flawless appearance to metal surfaces such as stainless steel, aluminum, copper, brass, etc. with abrasive equipment.

Satination: is a surface application aimed to add a matte finish to metals.

Superficial processes are provided within the SCZ Metal Welding section. We can apply Polishing and Satin to all material surfaces. Surface roughness depends on customer demand.
