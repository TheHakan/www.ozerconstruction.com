WHAT IS CNC ABKANT PRESS?
CNC Abkant Press twists materials into the desired shape. This method allows for multiple twists and is suitable for mass production.

Thanks to our CNC Abkant Press machines, (high quality,) precise bending is faster and easier than ever for all kinds of metal plates.
