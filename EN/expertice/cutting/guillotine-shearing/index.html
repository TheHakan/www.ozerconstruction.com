WHAT IS GUILLOTINE SHEARING?
The method of cutting where hard to cut metal sheets are broken off from each other is called guillotine shearing. This method allows for the cutting of metal with absolute precision.

With our guillotine shearing machines, metal plates of varying sizes and thicknesses are cut with millimetric and error-free precision.
